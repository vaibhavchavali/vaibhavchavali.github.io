Master's Thesis

Coprime Sensor Arrays (CSAs) and Nested Arrays (NAs) consist of two interleaved subarrays with different sample spacings. To reduce the number of sensors, at least one of the subarrays is undersampled. There are several approaches to estimating the spatial power spectrum given a sparse set of CSA or NA measurements. The classical approach is to implement a multiplicative processor.
